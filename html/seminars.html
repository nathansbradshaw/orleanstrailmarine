Seminars

Once a month Cycle Fitness hosts Biking seminars to introduce, inform, and clarify information about bikes. We want our customers and fellow cyclist to practice safety, proper care of bikes and to have a definite understanding on what bike would serve them the best. We want all cyclists to know how to safely navigate the roads, how to fix common problems on their bikes and how they can keep their bike in the best condition.
