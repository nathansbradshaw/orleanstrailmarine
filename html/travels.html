Tour De New York 2015


Allegheny River Valley Trail

Planned: January 31, 2015

This is a 5.6 mile trail paved with asphalt. The trail runs along the Allegheny River and is the perfect way to start the New Year.

Auburn Trail

Planned: February 28, 2015

The Auburn Trail is 9.1 miles long located in the town of Victor, Ontario County. The trail is mainly a stone trail and is fairly flat.

Genesee Riverway Trail

Planned: March 28, 2015

This trail is Asphalt making it great for road bikes. The trail itself is 16.5 miles that travels along the beach and into the city.

Ballston Veterans Bike Path

Planned: April 25, 2015

The Ballston Trail is perfect for a family trips or for amateur bikers because it is only 3.5 miles long. The trail is asphalt with forest on either side of you.

Bethpage Bikeway

Planned: May 30, 2015

This trail may be 12.5 miles but don't worry if you think you can't make it because there are several exits along the way.

Black Bridge Trail

Planned: June 27, 2015

Black Bridge Trail has a beautiful scenery and is very short, it is the perfect trail for any nature lover.

Black River Feeder Canal Trail

Planned: July 25, 2015

This trail follows the canal for 10 miles. The trail itself is rocky but it has a great view of the Black River Canal.

Bronx River Greenway

Planned: August 29, 2015

The Bronx River Greenway is an 18 mile trail with some paved sections. The trail show nature along with New York history.

Catharine Valley Trail

Planned: September 26, 2015

This is an amazing way to get to know nature with this 12 mile gravel trail.
